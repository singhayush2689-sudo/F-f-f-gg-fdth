About Burger Town

Our Story

Established in 2015, Burger Town has been serving the finest burgers across India. What started as a small food truck in Mumbai has now grown into a beloved chain with locations in major cities across the country.

Our mission is simple: to create the perfect burger experience. We believe that a great burger starts with quality ingredients, innovative recipes, and a passion for food that brings people together.
